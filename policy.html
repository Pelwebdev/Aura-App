Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Aura Conect Limited ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal information when you access or use our platform (including the website, mobile app, and all related services). By using the Aura Conect platform, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide:

Registration: When you create an account, we may collect your name, email address, date of birth, and other information you choose to provide.

Usage Data: We collect information about your use of the platform, such as the features you access, the content you interact with, and the duration of your sessions.

Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the platform, such as your device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers.

Location Data: If you enable location services, we may collect information about your location.

Information Collected Automatically:

Cookies and Similar Technologies: We may use cookies, web beacons, and other similar technologies to collect information about your use of the platform..

Log Files: We may collect information that your browser sends whenever you visit our website, such as your browser type, language preferences, and IP address.

How We Use Your Information

Provide and Improve Services: We use your information to provide and improve the Aura Conect platform, including personalizing your experience, delivering content, and providing customer support.

Communicate with You: We may use your information to communicate with you about the platform, including sending you notifications, updates, and marketing messages.

Conduct Research and Analysis: We may use your information to conduct research and analysis to improve our services and develop new features.

Comply with Legal Obligations: We may use your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to court orders or law enforcement requests.

Data Sharing

We may share your information with:

Service Providers: Third-party service providers who assist us in providing and improving the platform, such as data analytics providers, cloud service providers, and marketing agencies.

Business Partners: Carefully selected business partners who may offer services or products that may be of interest to you.

Legal and Law Enforcement: Law enforcement or other government officials as required by law or when we believe disclosure is necessary to protect our rights, protect your safety or the safety of others, investigate fraud, or respond to a legal request.

We may also share aggregated or anonymized data with third parties for research or marketing purposes.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, use, or disclosure. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is completely secure. Therefore, we cannot guarantee the absolute security of your information.

Your Choices

You can control certain aspects of how we collect and use your information by:

Updating Your Account Settings: You can access and update your account information and privacy settings within the app.

Managing Cookies: You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Opting Out of Marketing Communications: You can opt out of receiving marketing communications from us by following the unsubscribe instructions provided in the communication.

Children's Privacy

The Aura Conect platform is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that we may have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes to this Privacy Policy by posting the revised Privacy Policy on the platform and updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
